Research focus

My research pairs sociology and ecology to address biodiversity declines and climate change by studying factors mediating insect communities and soil microbiomes within agroecosystems.

Research interests: Quantitative ecology, socio-ecological models, community science, sustainable agriculture, temporal and spatial analysis, decision support tools, policy
